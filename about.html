About

The Memoir

The Memoir is a tastefully crafted stay with a waft of class that sets it apart. A Guest at the Memoir is truly a guest of the house and that, we swear by our art of giving. The Memoir is a boutique hotel that would stay etched in the memories of our Guests. We composed and furnished each room with an earthy taste, a minimalist layout and ample natural light. With an option to extend the stay to work, The Memoir provides workstations and conference rooms for businesspeople. A perfect stay for our revered guests with a flair for panache. Not to overlook the fuel for the body, Barandah elevates the experience at The Memoir with its sumptuous menus served in rooms or at the restaurant.


About us

An offering of a tea seller, that started in the 1940s as Green Lodge, traversing through the genes and decades of aridity, springs back to life. 2020 marked the sprouting of The Memoir.
